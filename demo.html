oa — interactive demo

A recorded terminal session of the OpenAlex semantic-search CLI. Press play, pause, seek — the text is selectable and copyable.

Try it yourself

uv venv --python 3.14 .venv
uv pip install --python .venv/bin/python -e .
.venv/bin/oa search "graph neural networks for drug discovery" -n 10

Full instructions in the README. A live, in-browser terminal (ttyd) is also available — see examples/web-terminal.
